I don't just analyse data — I tell its story.

Growing up fascinated by patterns — in mathematics, in design, in human behaviour — I found my calling at the crossroads of data and creativity. When numbers alone feel cold and interfaces feel like noise, I ask: what does this actually mean for a person?
